Our Coffee Services

Coffee is a globally cherished beverage brewed from roasted seeds — commonly called beans — of the Coffea plant. With its deep aroma, energizing properties, and bold flavor, coffee has become more than just a drink; it's a cultural phenomenon that connects people across the world.Coffee traces its roots back to the ancient coffee forests of Ethiopia, where legend says a goat herder named Kaldi discovered the energizing effects of coffee beans. From there, coffee spread to the Middle East, especially Yemen, where the first known coffeehouses — known as qahveh khaneh — were established.By the 17th century, coffee had reached Europe and rapidly gained popularity, eventually making its way to the Americas. Today, it’s grown in over 70 countries along the “Coffee Belt” — regions near the equator with the perfect climate for cultivating coffee.

Coffee is brewed from roasted seeds of the Coffea plant. It contains caffeine, a natural stimulant that enhances alertness and focus. With over 800 flavor compounds, coffee offers diverse tasting notes—from fruity and floral to nutty and chocolaty.Coffee is a rich, aromatic beverage made from roasted coffee beans. Known for its energizing caffeine content, it’s enjoyed in countless forms—from strong espressos to smooth lattes. Whether hot or iced, coffee is the perfect companion for early mornings late-night conversations.
